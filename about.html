Welcome to MARON Real Estate Group

MARON Real Estate Group stands as a beacon of luxury and innovation in Kericho's property development landscape. With a commitment to excellence that spans every project, we have redefined what it means to create truly exceptional living spaces.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to elevate the standard of living in Kericho through meticulously designed properties that blend modern aesthetics with practical functionality. Today, we continue to push boundaries, creating homes that inspire and communities that thrive.

By aiming to take the life quality to an upper level with all realized projects, MARON continues to be the address of luxury, setting new benchmarks in design, construction quality, and customer satisfaction.
